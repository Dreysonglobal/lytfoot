Data catalogue

Unify your data catalogue

A single source of truth for seamless integration, discovery and decision making on datasets and vendors.

  • Catalogue your own data: Add your vendors, datasets, brokers, service providers, and other custom data assets you manage
  • Synchronised with Neudata's library: Realtime updates to your catalogue from Neudata's managed library of over 8,000 alternative and market data products
  • Flexible integrations: Keep your key systems in place using Navigator's custom integrations

Trial

Collaboratively manage your data trials

Conduct data trials as projects, define your assessment criteria, assign and collaborate on tasks and compare products

  • Set your data objectives: Capture business goals, assign sponsors, and define key data criteria to achieve them
  • Evaluate data products: Select potential datasets and collaborate on assessment activities
  • Onboarding decisions: Rank datasets based on your assessment criteria ‚Äî approve or reject products for use

Subscriptions

Capture subscriptions, contracts and negotiations

Manage your data product subscriptions, represent contracts and save money during renewals

  • Create subscriptions: Create contracts, capture key dates, license terms, cost breakdown, and setup notifications
  • Manage licenses: Setup users under individual user, enterprise, pay as you go, or credit based license models
  • Negotiate renewals: Summarise vendor relationships, revisit past negotiations, price comparable datasets and capture outcomes
  • Report spend: Allocate invoices, track payments, assign cost per user, and summarise costs in your selected currency

Risk

Identify, mitigate and reduce risk

Uncover and mitigate risks across vendors, datasets and trials. Follow up with actions and escalations.

  • Custom risk capture: Define risk categories, provide definitions, and set impact levels according to your risk tolerance
  • Make risk assessments: Capture risk assessments on vendors, datasets and custom data assets when performing due diligence
  • Summarise risks: Report on risk based on category, impact level and share with key stakeholders
  • Follow-up actions: Remediate or mitigate risks by assigning users to take action

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Navigator

Can I use Navigator with my existing data catalogue or payment system? +

Yes, Navigator is designed to integrate with your existing systems. Our flexible API allows seamless connection with data catalogues, payment systems, and other enterprise software.

Can I subscribe to certain modules within Navigator such as Trial Management? +

Absolutely. Navigator is modular by design, allowing you to subscribe only to the components you need, whether it's Trial Management, Data Catalogue, Subscriptions, or Risk Management.

Can I import my data content from existing systems? +

Yes, we provide data migration tools and services to help you import your existing data content, vendor information, contracts, and other relevant data into Navigator.

Does Navigator integrate with my Scout/Ranger subscriptions? +

Yes, Navigator seamlessly integrates with Neudata's Scout and Ranger subscriptions, providing a unified platform for data discovery, evaluation, and management.
